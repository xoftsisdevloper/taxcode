GST Services

Complete GST solutions including registration, return filing, compliance, and notice handling. We ensure your business stays fully compliant with GST laws without stress.

What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services in India. Businesses must register under GST once they cross the prescribed turnover limit or fall under mandatory categories.

Why is GST Important?
  • Mandatory legal compliance for businesses
  • Enables input tax credit benefits
  • Builds credibility with customers
  • Avoid heavy penalties and notices
  • Required for e-commerce sellers
Who Needs GST?
  • Businesses with turnover above ₹40 Lakhs (₹20 Lakhs for services)
  • E-commerce sellers (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)
  • Interstate suppliers
  • Freelancers and service providers
  • Startups and growing businesses
When Should You Register?

GST registration should be done before starting taxable sales, crossing turnover limits, or selling on online platforms.

Where is GST Registered?

GST registration is done online through the GST Portal under the Government of India.

Documents Required
  • PAN Card of business / owner
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Business address proof
  • Electricity bill / rent agreement
  • Bank account details
  • Passport size photo
  • Digital Signature (for companies/LLP)
